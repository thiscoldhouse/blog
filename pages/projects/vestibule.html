The Vestibule

I'm not sure that "Vestibule" is the right name for it, but it's so much more exciting than mudroom I think we're going to run with it. I suppose entryway would probably work too. Anyway, here's the vestibule:

The motivation behind the vestibule was many fold, it's partly an installment of the This Warm House series, in which I foolishly attempt to weatherproof the house, and it's partly because there was no real entryway - as the house stands now, the front door opens directly into the living room. We thought it'd be pleasant to have something attractive to serve as a barrier between our living room and the elements.

As I'm sure will continue to be a theme, there's still a bit left to do. It's currently only primed, not painted. Since we'll be painting the house in the spring and we're not quite settled on colors yet, it seemed wise to do it all in one go.
